# SpatiumTempus — The Stolen Time

SpatiumTempus is a narrated visual presentation that transforms special-relativistic time dilation into a detective mystery: **The Stolen Time — The Case of the Missing 12 Seconds**.

## The Story

Einstein steals an atomic clock and escapes from Earth at **0.99c**. He travels to Plasma Square Space Station, remains there briefly, and returns to Earth at the same speed.

The police catch him with the clock and bring him before Newton, who investigates the apparent disappearance of twelve seconds. Their confrontation uses clocks, motion, evidence, and dialogue to explain why the travelling clock records less elapsed time.

## What It Explains

The project introduces:

- Special-relativistic time dilation
- Proper time
- Relative motion
- Clock comparison
- The effect of travelling at 0.99c
- Why elapsed time can differ without time literally being stolen
